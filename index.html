How I work

The Process

01

Concept

Every piece begins with an idea — a texture, a form, a feeling. I sketch and experiment before touching clay, letting the concept guide the medium.

02

Form

Whether handbuilt, wheel-thrown, or sculpted in polymer, I work slowly — letting the material push back and inform the final shape.

03

Finish

Glaze, texture, and surface detail are where each piece finds its personality. I favor earthy palettes and organic surfaces that age beautifully.
